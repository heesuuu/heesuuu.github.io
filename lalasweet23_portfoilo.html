Lalaswet has grown into a leading brand in the low-calorie ice cream market since its inception in 2017 through crowdfunding.
In 2021, the brand expanded its offerings beyond pint ice cream with the introduction of a new line of mochi ice cream.

Alongside this growth, I led a brand renewal effort to establish Lalasweet as a healthy ice cream brand. This involved creating new symbols, logos, and brand assets to cater to customers who want to enjoy ice cream without worrying about calories or junk food.
Additionally, I developed a package system and guidelines to capture attention in offline stores.
Lalasweet will now be remembered not just for its low-calorie pint ice cream but as a healthy ice cream brand.
