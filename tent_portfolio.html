2020 Winter Promotion
Lalasweet
2020. 10 - 2020. 11
Role

Project Manager
Illustration (100%)
Leaflet Design (100%)

Lalasweet aims to deliver a variety of experiences beyond just being perceived as a food brand.
To provide customers with a cozy space to enjoy sweet treats on cold winter days, I created Lalasweet Winter Heating Tents and blankets.
I used materials, colors, and graphics that align with the brand's tone and manner to allow customers to indirectly experience brand.
The event, held in line with the winter season of 2020, sold out within a day of opening.
I oversaw the entire process from contacting the production company to directing the design and production.
